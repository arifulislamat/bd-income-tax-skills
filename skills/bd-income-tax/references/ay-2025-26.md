# AY 2025-26 — parameters (prior year, for late/prior returns)

**Income year:** 1 Jul 2024 – 30 Jun 2025 → **Assessment Year 2025-26**.
**Governing law:** Income Tax Act 2023 as amended by the **Finance Ordinance 2025** (enacted
by the interim government with Parliament dissolved). `[ITA-2023]` `[FO-2025]`

All figures are in BDT. Numbers feed `scripts/tax_calc.py` — never hand-calculate.

## General individual slab `[FO-2025]`

| Income band | Cumulative upper bound | Rate |
|---|---|---|
| First 350,000 | 350,000 | 0% |
| Next 100,000 | 450,000 | 5% |
| Next 400,000 | 850,000 | 10% |
| Next 500,000 | 1,350,000 | 15% |
| Next 500,000 | 1,850,000 | 20% |
| Next 2,000,000 | 3,850,000 | 25% |
| Balance | — | 30% |

> The **5% slab still applies** this year (abolished from AY 2026-27). At the same income,
> AY 2025-26 tax differs from AY 2026-27 — e.g. total income 880,000 (Dhaka/Ctg CC) →
> **49,500** here vs **60,750** under AY 2026-27.

The slab widths apply to income **above** the taxpayer's tax-free threshold below.

## Salary exemption (employment head) `[ITA-2023]` (6th Sch., Part A, para 27)

Exempt the **lower of** one-third of total salary income or the annual cap.
- **AY 2025-26 cap: BDT 450,000.**

## Exempt end-of-service benefits `[ITA-2023-6Sch]`

Same as AY 2026-27: **gratuity** from a government or NBR-approved fund is exempt up to
**BDT 2.5 crore** (Sixth Schedule Part 1, paras 5–6); the excess is taxable. See `ay-2026-27.md`.

## Investment tax rebate — Section 78

Same rule as AY 2026-27: rebate = 15% of the lowest of {3% of total income, 15% of eligible
investment, BDT 1,000,000}. No rebate if filed after Tax Day. See `ay-2026-27.md` for the
eligible-investment list. `[ITA-2023]` `[PwC]`

## Minimum tax (area-based — last year of this system) `[FO-2025]`

| Location (`tax_calc.py` key) | Minimum tax |
|---|---|
| Dhaka North, Dhaka South, Chattogram City Corporation (`dhaka_ctg_cc`) | 5,000 |
| Other city corporations (`other_cc`) | 4,000 |
| Outside city corporations (`other`) | 3,000 |
| New taxpayers (`is_new_taxpayer`) | 1,000 |

Gross-receipts minimum tax (0.25% where gross receipts > BDT 40,000,000) applies as in AY 2026-27.

Same bands as AY 2026-27 (see `ay-2026-27.md`). `[DailyStar]`
